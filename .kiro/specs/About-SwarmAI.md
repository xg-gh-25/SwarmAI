
# SwarmAI

## Tagline
**Your AI Team, 24/7**

## Slogan
**Work Smarter. Stress Less.**

## Product Description
SwarmAI is your personalized AI team, working 24/7 to turn your daily work into meaningful results.

Bring your communications — email, Slack, Teams — along with documents, meetings, research, planning, tasks, and reports into one intelligent workspace. SwarmAI assembles a supervised team of domain-specific agents and skills that plan, write, summarize, organize, coordinate, take action, and follow through.

SwarmAI builds long-term, persistent memory in your local desktop (only visible for you). Your AI team remembers context, preferences, past decisions, ongoing projects, and evolving priorities — so work doesn’t reset every day. The more you use it, the smarter and more aligned it becomes. SwarmAI doesn’t just respond — it remembers, adapts, and improves over time. Your AI team grows with you, building institutional memory instead of losing context.

Automatically assign work or delegate tasks to the right agent. They collaborate in the background, execute reliably, and continuously learn under your supervision.

No more switching tools. No more repeating yourself. No more chasing context.

You stay in control — guiding your AI team — while SwarmAI helps you deliver results faster, improve quality, and create lasting value with less stress.
