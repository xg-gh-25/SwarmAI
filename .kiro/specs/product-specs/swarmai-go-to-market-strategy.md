# 5. Flywheel Stage Design

## Stage 1 — Thought Leadership (Category Creation)

Goal: Make operators realize their work is *execution orchestration*, not just task tracking.

Key Narrative:

> “Your job is not managing tasks — it is orchestrating outcomes.
> SwarmAI is your AI execution team.”

Content Themes:

* Why PM work is actually coordination + synthesis
* Why marketing ops is mostly follow-up & reporting overhead
* Why managers need execution visibility, not more dashboards
* Why chat alone cannot run real work

Channels:

* LinkedIn (primary for PM/marketing/sales leaders)
* X (operator + startup community)
* Substack / Medium (deep essays)

Success Signal:

> Audience starts repeating: “AI work OS” / “AI execution team”

---

## Stage 2 — Operator Community Formation

Goal: Build a tight group of early adopters who use SwarmAI daily.

### Community Structure

Create:

> “AI Operators Circle”

Members:

* PMs
* Marketers
* Sales leads
* Strategy / Ops managers

Community Value:

* Share real workflows (not prompts)
* Show how AI agents execute recurring work
* Peer learning about delegation patterns

---

## Stage 3 — Workflow Template Flywheel

Goal: Turn individual usage into reusable public playbooks.

Examples:

* “Weekly PM Review Workflow”
* “Marketing Campaign Launch Orchestration”
* “Sales Pipeline Follow-up System”
* “Executive Weekly Report Generator”

Each template includes:

1. Input signals (emails, meetings, tasks)
2. ToDo triage logic
3. Multi-agent execution pattern
4. Output artifacts (report, plan, follow-ups)

Templates become:

> Repeatable “Operating Systems” for roles

---

## Stage 4 — Social Proof & Case Narratives

Goal: Let operators learn from peers, not product docs.

Example Stories:

* “How I replaced my weekly reporting workflow with SwarmAI agents”
* “Our marketing team uses SwarmAI as a campaign command center”
* “As a PM, I finally stopped chasing follow-ups manually”

Key Rule:

> Always tell outcome stories, never feature stories.

---

## Stage 5 — Peer Recommendation Loop

Operators trust:

* Other PMs
* Other marketers
* Other sales leaders

More than:

* Engineers
* Technical influencers

So the viral unit becomes:

> “Operator to operator recommendation”

Example:

> “You’re drowning in coordination? You should try SwarmAI — it runs my weekly work automatically.”
