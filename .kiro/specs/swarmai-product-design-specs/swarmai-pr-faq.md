## FAQ

### 1. What is SwarmAI?

SwarmAI is a persistent AI command center where a supervised AI team continuously triages incoming work, executes tasks, and produces durable outputs inside a single workspace.

It’s designed for real-world knowledge work — not just answering questions, but helping you follow through on emails, reports, planning, and cross-team coordination over time.

---

- grounded reasoning, or

SwarmAI unifies these layers into a single system that continuously executes and follows through on work inside a persistent workspace.

### 4. Who is it for?

SwarmAI is designed for knowledge workers who manage ongoing, multi-channel workloads, especially:
- Marketing leaders running campaigns and reports

- Business managers tracking cross-team actions and outcomes

---

### 5. What problems does it solve?

SwarmAI addresses three core pains:
1. Work scattered across email, chat, docs, and meetings
2. AI sessions that reset and lose context
3. Tasks that start but don’t get followed through

SwarmAI keeps context persistent, tracks signals as structured ToDos, executes tasks with agents, and ensures outcomes are completed and captured as reusable artifacts.

---

### 6. How does it actually work?

At a high level:

```

Signals (emails, chats, meetings) → ToDos → Tasks → Agent Execution → Outcome + Artifact

```

Users can:
- drag or click ToDos into execution
- supervise plans before execution (when needed)
- provide input mid-execution
- review outcomes when risk or importance requires it

Everything remains traceable and resumable inside the workspace.

---

### 7. Does it replace existing tools?

No. SwarmAI is designed to **orchestrate across existing tools**, not replace them.

It sits above:
- Email and messaging platforms
- Project and task systems
- Document and knowledge tools

SwarmAI ingests signals, executes structured work, and then replies or updates back to the original channels.

- planning work

- following up and closing loops

You remain in control, but the agents handle the ongoing execution and follow-through continuously in the background.
